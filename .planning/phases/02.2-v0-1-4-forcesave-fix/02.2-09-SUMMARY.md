## Decisions Made

1. **#### sub-heading depth (one level below ADR-8's ###).** Expresses the "refinement of" relationship at the typographic level. ADR-1..8 stay as top-level architectural decisions; ADR-8.1 is subordinated under ADR-8 in the rendered Markdown TOC.
2. **Cite test contracts by exact name.** Future contributors reading spec.md cold can grep for `test_merge_fields_omits_privacy_password` (or any cited name) and immediately find the regression-locking unit/integration test. Names match what Plan 07 RED + Plan 08 GREEN actually committed.
3. **Two-layer Conséquences enumeration.** The defense-in-depth stance is the load-bearing architectural insight from D-02.2-AUTH-REGRESSION. Documenting it explicitly (rather than leaving it implicit) prevents a future contributor from "simplifying" by removing one layer (which would re-open one of two distinct regression classes).
4. **qBit/Jellyfin out-of-scope clause restated for ADR-8.1.** ADR-8 already had a qBit/Jellyfin out-of-scope clause for the HTTP layer; ADR-8.1 adds the merge-layer equivalent. Future contributors planning Phase 5/7 will see both layers' boundaries in one place.
5. **Cross-reference paths use full `.planning/phases/02.2-v0-1-4-forcesave-fix/...` form** rather than relative paths. spec.md is consumed by `gsd-import` from the project root; absolute-from-root paths are unambiguous.
