8. `diff /tmp/qBittorrent.conf.snapshot-bak /config/qBittorrent/qBittorrent.conf` → exit 0 (DIFF CLEAN: bit-identical)
9. `s6-svc -u` — restart qBit on original whitelist; verified `pgrep qbittorrent-nox` returns pid
